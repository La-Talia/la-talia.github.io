ABOUT ME

WHO
AM I

I'm Raxit Gupta — a first-year BTech student at IIT ISM Dhanbad, known online as LATALIA. I live at the intersection of code and hardware, splitting time between competitive programming arenas, ML experiments, robotics builds, and CAD workbenches.

This site is my public notebook. Every guide here exists because I got stuck on that exact topic and couldn't find a clear explanation. If it helps someone get unstuck, it was worth writing.

On the CP side I compete on Codeforces using a custom C++ template with Python ergonomics. On the ML side I explore neural architectures. On the robotics and CAD side, I build things that move.
