ABOUT ME

WHO
AM I

I'm Raxit Gupta — a first-year BTech student at IIT ISM Dhanbad, known online as LATALIA. I live at the intersection of code and hardware, splitting my time between competitive programming arenas, machine learning experiments, robotics builds, and CAD workbenches.

This site is my public notebook — every guide here exists because I got stuck on that exact topic and couldn't find a clear explanation. If it helps someone get unstuck, it was worth writing.

On the CP side, I compete on Codeforces using a custom C++ template that brings Python's ergonomics to contest-speed code. On the ML side, I explore neural architectures and practical deep learning. On the robotics and CAD side, I build things that move.
